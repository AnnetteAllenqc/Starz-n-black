Starz-N-Black

A nonprofit organization based in Davenport, Iowa, serving the Quad Cities and surrounding areas through community outreach, compassion, and action.

Support the Mission Join the Team

Our Mission

Starz-N-Black is dedicated to giving back to our community by providing hot meals for the homeless, food pantry support, and resources for families in need. We believe in unity, dignity, and making a lasting impact.

What We Do

Hot Meals

Providing warm, nourishing meals to individuals experiencing homelessness.

Food Pantry

Distributing food and essentials to families in the Quad Cities area.

Community Outreach

Supporting local events, families, and individuals in need.

Making a Difference Together

With the help of volunteers, donors, and community partners, Starz-N-Black continues to shine light where it’s needed most.
